# Source Watchlist

This file tracks source categories for AI business adoption and market intelligence briefs.

## AI and technology company sources

Use for product direction, implementation examples, and vendor claims.

Treat as: useful but biased primary evidence.

## BI and data workflow sources

Use for understanding business intelligence, dashboards, automation, analytics workflows, and data-stack changes.

Treat as: practical workflow evidence, but often vendor-led.

## FMCG, retail, sales, and distribution sources

Use for sector relevance, operational pressures, supply chain issues, buyer behaviour, and distribution trends.

Treat as: sector context.

## Official and policy sources

Use for productivity, SME adoption, regulation, digital strategy, economic context, and regional business conditions.

Treat as: credibility and context, but often broad rather than operational.

## Professional insight sources

Use for frameworks, implementation lessons, and business adoption patterns.

Treat as: useful framing, but check for consulting or marketing bias.

## Independent news sources

Use for current market signals and company activity.

Treat as: useful discovery, but verify important claims.
