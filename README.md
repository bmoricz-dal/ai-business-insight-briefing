# AI Business Insight Briefing

A lightweight research workflow for producing practical AI adoption and market intelligence briefs.

## Purpose

This repo turns selected business and technology signals into short analyst-style briefs.

The focus is not to track every AI headline. The focus is to understand how organisations use AI, data, business intelligence, and workflow automation to improve decisions, operations, and measurable business value.

## What this project covers

- AI adoption in real business settings
- Business intelligence and workflow automation
- FMCG, sales, distribution, and operational relevance
- SME-facing AI and software services
- Market intelligence and implementation signals
- Source quality, bias checks, and practical business interpretation

## What this project does not cover

- Generic AI news summaries
- Academic literature reviews
- Investment advice
- Vendor claims treated as neutral proof
- Private or personal career notes

## Core question

What does this business or AI signal teach about real implementation, workflow change, market opportunity, or better decision-making?

## Output format

Each brief should include:

1. Short version
2. Best business signal
3. AI implementation case
4. Market intelligence note
5. FMCG / sales / distribution angle
6. Method or framework explained simply
7. Practical takeaway
8. Follow-up research opportunity

## Source quality rules

Sources should be treated carefully.

Vendor sources can show useful signals, but they are not neutral proof. Official sources, reputable industry sources, and independent reporting should be used where possible.

Each brief should separate:

- Fact: what the source says
- Meaning: what it likely means
- Risk: what may be exaggerated or uncertain
- Application: how it could matter in practice
